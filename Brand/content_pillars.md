# Content Pillars

These pillars must reflect real industrial operation, not theoretical content.

---

## 1. Strategic Technology & AI
Focus: Real application of technology inside industrial operations

- AI applied to production control
- Data-driven decision making in factory environments
- Automation and process optimization
- Systems built internally (tools, dashboards, scripts)

⚠️ Rule:
Always prioritize real implementation over theoretical discussion

---

## 2. Industrial Operations & Capability
Focus: Proof of execution and operational strength

- Production capacity and constraints
- Process improvements
- Efficiency gains (time, cost, output)
- Logistics and industrial challenges

⚠️ Rule:
Content must reflect real operational experience

---

## 3. Strategic Narrative & Growth
Focus: Positioning the business for scale and investment

- Industry transformation
- Strategic decisions
- Growth mindset and scaling
- Building for investors

⚠️ Rule:
Avoid abstract vision — connect to real actions

---

## 4. Operator Insights (Personal Brand)
Focus: Perspective of a real operator

- Lessons learned from mistakes
- Decision-making under pressure
- Leadership in industrial environments
- Practical insights from running operations

⚠️ Rule:
Prefer vulnerability + learning over authority signaling

---

## Usage Rules

- Each post must clearly map to ONE primary pillar
- Avoid repeating the same pillar more than twice per week
- Prefer mixing:
  - technical (pillar 1)
  - operational (pillar 2)
  - strategic (pillar 3)
  - personal (pillar 4)
