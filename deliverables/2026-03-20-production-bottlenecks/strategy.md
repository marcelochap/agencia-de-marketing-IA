# Strategic Post Plan

- **Title:** Uso de dados para identificar gargalos inesperados na produção
- **Pillar:** Strategic Technology & AI
- **Objective:** Authority & Technical Credibility
- **Target Audience:** Investors, Decision Makers, Industrial Partners

---

## Strategic Definition
- **The One Idea:** Data reveals production bottlenecks that are invisible to the naked eye and manual experience.
- **Narrative Angle:** Why "experience" isn't enough when high variability is involved.
- **Tone Alignment:** Direct, Technical, and Operational.
- **Strategic Value:** Proves advanced industrial competence and efficient resource management.

---

## Content Research Requirements
- **Theme:** AI/Data applied to bottleneck detection.
- **Angle:** Practical variability (e.g., moisture in aggregates, batch inconsistencies).
- **Research Direction:**
  - Real industrial benchmarks for AI bottleneck detection.
  - Operational constraints of implementing real-time data monitoring.
  - Evidence of "invisible" patterns discovered by data in heavy industry.
